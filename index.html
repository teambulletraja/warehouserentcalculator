How figures are calculated: House property income = gross annual rent × 70% (30% std deduction, Sec 24(a)). New regime (Budget 2025, FY 2025-26): ₹0–4L nil, ₹4–8L 5%, ₹8–12L 10%, ₹12–16L 15%, ₹16–20L 20%, ₹20–24L 25%, above ₹24L 30%. Sec 87A rebate: income ≤₹12L → nil tax. Old regime: 87A if ≤₹5L; senior (60–79) ₹3L exemption; super senior (80+) ₹5L exemption. Cess 4%. GST threshold ₹20L/yr; RCM under Notification 05/2022. No municipal tax deduction assumed. Parent always holds W-A (higher rent, GST registered). Child holds W-B (lower rent, benefits from RCM while below threshold).
