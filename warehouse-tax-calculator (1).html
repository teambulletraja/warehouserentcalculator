How figures are calculated: House property income = gross annual rent × 70% (30% std deduction, Sec 24(a)). New regime (Budget 2025, FY 2025-26): ₹0–4L nil, ₹4–8L 5%, ₹8–12L 10%, ₹12–16L 15%, ₹16–20L 20%, ₹20–24L 25%, above ₹24L 30%. Sec 87A rebate: income ≤₹12L → nil tax. Old regime: 87A if income ≤₹5L; senior (60–79) exemption ₹3L; super senior (80+) exemption ₹5L. Cess 4%. GST threshold ₹20L/yr; RCM under Notification 05/2022. No municipal tax deduction assumed.
